Common questions

Before you call.

Most of what people ask before scheduling. Don't see your question? Call us — we'd rather answer in two minutes than have you wait.

How long does a roof replacement take?
Most asphalt jobs: 1–2 days. Concrete tile: 3–5 days. Metal: 2–3 days. We work full days from 7 AM, not weeks of half-shifts. You'll know the exact schedule before we start.
Will my insurance cover storm damage?
If your roof has hail or wind damage AND it's within your policy's claim window (almost always one year from the storm date), yes. We document the damage, meet your adjuster on-site, and handle the claim paperwork. You pay only your deductible — the depreciation check stays with you.
What if I just need a small repair, not a replacement?
We do repairs every day. Minimum service call is $250 (covers up to 90 minutes of work plus standard materials). For anything bigger, you get a written quote before we start.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. RCAT-certified (Roofing Contractors Association of Texas), license #100-4821-2024. Fully insured with $2M general liability and workers' comp on every crew. Certificates available on request.
How much does a new roof cost?
Average asphalt replacement in Central Texas: $11,400–$22,000 depending on square footage, pitch, and material grade. We give written quotes after inspection — no surprises, no upsells after the contract is signed.
Do you offer financing?
Yes. 0% APR for 12 months through GreenSky. Approval in minutes via a soft credit pull. Longer terms (24–60 months) available at standard rates.
What if I'm outside your service area?
Call us anyway — if it's within reasonable driving distance and the job is big enough, we can usually make it work. Worst case we'll recommend a contractor we trust in your area.
