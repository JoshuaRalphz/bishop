Two brothers, fourteen years, one company.

Bishop Roofing started after a hailstorm wrecked our parents' Cedar Park home in 2009 and three roofers in a row took the insurance money and disappeared. We started Bishop Roofing in 2010 to be the company we wished they'd had.

Our story

The roofers we wish our parents had hired.

In May 2009 a hailstorm flattened Cedar Park. Our parents' roof was destroyed. Their insurance paid out $18,000. Three different contractors took deposits, two never showed up, and the third did the job so poorly it leaked the first time it rained.

Travis was running a small framing crew in Round Rock at the time. Cole was finishing his last year of business school at Texas State. We spent the next six months learning roofing the hard way — certifications, ride-alongs with the few honest contractors in the area, and a stack of GAF manuals taller than the coffee table.

In March 2010 we filed for an LLC, bought a used F-250, and put our first sign in the front yard of our parents' newly repaired roof. Fourteen years later, the truck is different but the rule is the same: we answer the phone, we show up when we said we would, and we don't leave until the work is done right.

— Travis & Cole Bishop

Our timeline

Fourteen years, slowly.

We've never wanted to be the biggest roofer in Texas. We want to be the one your neighbor recommends without hesitating. Here's how we got from a used F-250 to here.

2010
Bishop Roofing LLC formed
Travis and Cole file in March. First job is a neighbor's torn-off porch overhang. 4 jobs total in year one.
2013
First crew hired
Brought on three full-time installers. Stopped contracting day labor. The work quality jumped overnight.
2015
GAF Master Elite certified
Joined the top 2% of US roofing contractors. Required to maintain training, customer satisfaction scores, and an extensive insurance bond.
2017
Insurance-claim specialty formalized
After three years of accidentally becoming the regional storm-claim experts, Cole completed Texas Department of Insurance training to formally represent homeowners.
2019
First million-dollar year
130 roofs replaced. Hired our first full-time office staff. Still using the original F-250 (eventually retired in 2022, now parked outside the Cedar Park shop).
2021
A+ BBB rating
Better Business Bureau accreditation. Zero unresolved complaints in 5 years of operation. Maintained ever since.
2024
RCAT certification
Joined the Roofing Contractors Association of Texas. License #100-4821-2024. State-recognized for code-compliance and insurance work.
2026
2,400+ roofs done
14 years in. 22 people on the team. Same two brothers running it. Still answering the phone.
