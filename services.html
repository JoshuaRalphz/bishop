Roof Replacement

Asphalt · Metal · Tile
Typical Investment
$11,400 – $22,000

Full tear-off down to the deck, inspection of the underlying structure, new underlayment, drip edge, flashing, and your chosen roofing material installed by our own crew.   • Synthetic underlayment standard, ice & water shield on every valley
  • Magnetic sweep of the entire property after the last truck leaves

Storm Damage & Insurance Claims

Hail · Wind · Fallen Debris
Your Cost
$0 out of pocket on most claims

This is what we built Bishop Roofing around. Texas insurance pays for hail and wind damage — but only if you document it correctly and file inside the policy window. Most homeowners don't know they have a claim until it's too late, or they file alone and get lowballed by $10k+.

We inspect first — for free, with drone photos and a written report. If there's damage, we walk your adjuster across the roof in person, document every hit, and re-walk the slope if anything gets missed on the first pass. You get the depreciation check when the work is done.

  • Free pre-claim inspection (no obligation, no contract)
  • Drone photo documentation timestamped and stored
  • On-site adjuster meeting — we don't leave you to argue alone
  • Direct billing to your insurer where the carrier supports it
  • We handle re-inspections, supplements, and appeals
  • $4.2M recovered for Central Texas homeowners since 2010

Free Inspections

48-Hour Response · Drone-Equipped
Cost
Free · No obligation

You call — we're on your roof within two business days. The inspection takes 45–60 minutes. We walk every slope (or fly the drone if the pitch is too steep), check the deck condition through the soffit, document any damage with photos, and check your gutters, flashing, and exterior penetrations while we're up there.

You walk away with a written PDF report, drone images, and a written quote — whether you hire us or not. No sales pressure, no "today only" pricing. If we don't think you need a new roof, we'll tell you.

  • Full PDF damage report delivered within 24 hours of inspection
  • Drone photo set (top-down + every slope)
  • Written quote with itemized line items, no upcharges later
  • Honest second opinion if you've already gotten quotes
  • We'll tell you when a repair makes more sense than a replacement

Gutters & Exterior

Seamless Gutters · Soffit · Fascia
Typical Investment
$1,800 – $4,200

A new roof on top of failing gutters is money down the drain — literally. We install seamless aluminum gutters on-site (no factory seams), repair or replace soffit and fascia, patch siding where storms pulled it loose, and add leaf guards if you want them. Same crew, same warranty structure as the roof above it.

  • 5" and 6" seamless aluminum gutters, baked-enamel finish
  • Color matched to your existing trim (28 standard colors)
  • Hidden hangers every 24" — not the sagging strap kind
  • Soffit & fascia repair, full or partial replacement
  • Optional leaf guards: micro-mesh or screen, both lifetime warranty
